      Is this based on real science or is it made-up?
      The Natural Signature System is built on established neuroscience research — specifically the Braverman model and Christian Thibaudeau's Neurotyping system, which links dominant neurotransmitters (dopamine, serotonin, GABA, acetylcholine) to personality, behavior, and physical performance. This isn't astrology. It's applied neuroscience adapted for everyday health and fitness. Individual differences in response to training are well-documented in the scientific literature — Bouchard et al. (1999) demonstrated significant inter-individual variation in physiological adaptation to the same exercise protocol, with some individuals showing near-zero response to programs that produced strong results in others. The neurotyping layer explains why.
